TECH FESTS IN HIT



    #1 RIVIERA

    The students of HIT organise the annual fest called, Riviera, every year.
    The mega event comprises performances of HIT students as well as of guest artists.
    The Institution encourages cultural activities for students by organizing Annual Cultural Festival - Riviera. This is purely a cultural fest . Vulgarity is totally prohibited ; any form of obsecity may debare the participant from the contest. Participants are judged on the basis of costumes,design aesthetics,theme,walking stance and attitude.
